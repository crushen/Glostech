About Glostech IT Services

Here at Glostech we offer an honest and personal IT experience to every customer, and we want to help make IT as easy as possible for you.

We specialise in PC / Laptop repairs, and with over 8 years experience working with IT equipment, we will always go above and beyond to make sure we resolve your issues.

If you're based in Cheltenhan or Gloucestershire, we can pick up and deliver any equipment, and we can do this out of hours to avoid disruption to your day.

To see more about what we do, please have a look at the sections below.

Our Services & Solutions

PC / Laptop Repairs

We cover a wide range of PC and Laptop repairs such as screen replacements, hardware and software repairs and much more.

Please get in touch with your enquiry.

Custom Built PC’s

We can custom build a PC for any purpose, whether it’s a basic machine for home or office use, or a top end gaming PC.

Let us know your budget and your PC will be ready to use straight out of the box.

Servicing & Upgrades

If your machine isn’t running as fast as it once did, it doesn’t mean it needs replacing! Some new upgrades or a good clean up will give it the speed boost it needs.

Virus Removal & Protection

All is not lost if your machine is infected with a virus – we can remove most infections and get you up and running again.

Office Moves & Setups

We can get your new office connected and ready to go, and we even offer an out-of-hours service to avoid disruption to your working day.

Data Recovery

If your machine can’t be repaired, your data may still be salvageable - We are able to recover data from most PC’s and laptops if the hard drives are still working.
